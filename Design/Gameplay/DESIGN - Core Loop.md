# DESIGN - Core Loop

## Decision

**DECIDED:** The core loop is the classic **Shadowrunner for hire** cycle:

**Pickup → Meet → Legwork → Execute → Payout → Downtime →** (repeat)

Every run goes through these phases. Side content (NSFW venues, training, contact management, chip-dealing, lifestyle decay) happens in Downtime.

## Loop phases

### Pickup
- Fixer call, BBS job board hit, overheard lead, contact favor, or self-directed target.
- Briefly describes the job (client archetype, target tier, risk, estimated payout). Player accepts or declines.

### Meet
- Physical meeting with Mr./Ms. Johnson in a venue (bar, hotel, safe house).
- Dialogue with skill checks (Etiquette, Negotiation, Intimidation, Sense Motive).
- Negotiate payout, access leverage, extract truth from the cover story.
- Commit to the run or walk.

### Legwork
- Optional, but always available. Time-boxed by the run's deadline.
- Channels: talk to contacts, decker database searches, street knowledge checks, surveillance, venue visits (including NSFW venues — brothel conversations, BTL-studio intel, bar gossip).
- Yields: intel (target layout, guard rotations, alarm systems), gear access, safer entry points, skill-check bonuses during Execute, alternative run-paths unlocked.
- **NSFW as legwork:** an escort, a brothel madam, or a BTL-studio regular can be the best source of intel on a target. Sex work venues are a legitimate legwork channel.

### Execute
- Mission environment (one or more scenes).
- Paths must exist for each archetype: combat, stealth, hacking (Decker), magic, social.
- Player can blend paths; one party member = PC, other skills come from gear/spells/programs.
- Outcome is a state delta: target hit / not, collateral, alarms, NPCs dead/alive, loot taken.

### Payout
- Return to Johnson or remote handoff.
- Dialogue: delivered as agreed, short of spec, over-delivered, betrayed.
- Nuyen + karma + rep. Faction state updated.
- Consequences baked in: hostile factions remember, helped factions note the favor.

### Downtime
- Time passes; rent bill ticks.
- Karma spend (skill up, new spell, new program, initiation).
- Gear shopping (street doc, fixer, fence, corp store if legit enough).
- Contact calls (social maintenance, romance beats, favors owed).
- Venue hangout (bar, brothel, shooting range, chip den, matrix club).
- Side content: BTL consumption, drug use (with addiction risk), sex work side hustle, etc.
- Next Pickup triggers when ready (fixer call, BBS notification, contact tip-off).

## Cadence target (MVP)

- **One full loop in MVP = one run.** After Payout, the vertical slice ends.
- Post-MVP: runs chain; karma accrues; rep compounds; era drifts.

## Loop across archetypes

Each archetype expresses the loop differently:

- **Street Sam:** combat-favored Execute; gear-shopping heavy Downtime.
- **Physical Adept:** combat + stealth Execute; meditation Downtime.
- **Decker:** Matrix-favored Legwork and Execute; host sprawling; cyberdeck maintenance Downtime.
- **Full Mage / Shaman:** Magic-favored Legwork (Astral scouting) and Execute (spells, conjuring); lodge / totem Downtime.

## Loop across the NSFW pillar

NSFW content plugs into the loop at three points:

- **Legwork:** venue-based intel (brothel, BTL studio, sex club).
- **Execute:** some runs *are* NSFW-primary (escort a client, raid a rival studio, deliver contraband chips to an underground buyer).
- **Downtime:** sex scenes with contacts, drug use, BTL consumption, side-hustle sex work.

NSFW is never detachable from the core loop — you can opt out of a specific scene, but not from the economic / social systems that make sex work, BTL, and drugs part of the world.

## Economy loop (compressed)

```
Nuyen in:    runs · contact favors · street economy (sex work, chip sales, drug sales, loot fencing)
Nuyen out:   rent · food · gear · bribes · medical · drug habits · chip addictions · lifestyle
Karma in:    run completion · notable choices · training downtime
Karma out:   attribute raises · skill raises · spell learning · initiation
Rep in:      run success · faction-specific favors
Rep out:     burned contacts · faction betrayals · public infamy
```

Each loop should move all four currencies.

## Open questions

- Time pressure — how tight are run deadlines? (Legwork budget control.)
- Rent cadence — monthly as per SR1E, or looser?
- Do runs stack? Can the PC hold two job offers and pick?
- How does the loop handle extreme outcomes (PC killed, PC jailed, PC captured)?
- Do some NSFW scenes pay karma directly (romance beats), or only nuyen (sex work)?
