# DESIGN - Progression

## Decision

**DECIDED:** Karma is awarded primarily on **run completion** with smaller bonuses for **notable choices** (clever plays, favored outcomes, moral / dramatic beats). Progression pace is **slow / gritty** — roughly one attribute or skill raise every 2–4 runs. Mage initiation and major cyberware upgrades are 10+ run investments.

## Karma ledger

Karma is tracked separately from nuyen. Spent only on permanent character advancement — cannot buy gear or services.

### Karma sources

- **Run completion (primary):** base karma per run, scaled by target tier and complexity.
  - Street-tier run: **2–4 karma** baseline.
  - Gang-tier run: **3–5 karma**.
  - Corp mid-tier: **5–8 karma**.
  - Corp high-tier / AAA: **8–15 karma**.
  - MVP retrieval target: mid-tier (**~5 karma** on clean completion).
- **Notable-choice bonuses:** +1 karma for a clever resolution, non-lethal success when lethal was easier, a significant moral beat, a memorable failure. Capped at 1–2 bonuses per run.
- **Survival bonus:** small karma for completing a run that should've killed the PC.
- **Downtime activities:** 0 karma from normal downtime. Training / research in specific contexts gives karma over long periods (post-MVP content).

### No karma from:
- Random combat encounters (XP-farming guard).
- Ambient social encounters.
- Shopping / travel.
- NSFW scenes (romance beats affect loyalty, not karma — though some beats can include karma from a notable-choice angle).

## Karma spend

Costs mirror SR1E classic curves — increasing cost at higher ratings.

### Attributes
Raising an attribute from N to N+1:

| N → N+1 | Karma cost |
|---|---|
| 1 → 2 | 2 |
| 2 → 3 | 3 |
| 3 → 4 | 4 |
| 4 → 5 | 6 |
| 5 → 6 | 8 |
| 6 → 7 (metatype cap permitting) | 12 |
| 7 → 8 | 16 |
| 8 → 9 | 20 |
| 9 → 10 | 25 |

### Skills
Raising a skill from N to N+1 costs half the attribute curve (cheaper to improve).

### Specializations
+1 dice on a specific skill application costs 2 karma.

### Magic rating
Raising Magic from N to N+1 costs karma per attribute curve, **plus** requires an initiation ordeal for ratings past the natural cap (post-MVP content).

### Learning new spells / programs / adept powers
- **Spell:** 2 karma + teacher / library / downtime.
- **Program / utility:** 2 karma + coding downtime (Electronics check).
- **Adept power:** costs Power points, not karma — but raising Magic unlocks more Power points.

### Contacts
- **Buying a new contact:** 2–5 karma depending on Connection tier (1–2 cheap, 5–6 expensive).
- **Raising Loyalty** on existing contact: 1 karma per point (via trust-milestone scenes or scripted events, not free spend).

### Mid-MVP cadence
- Starting karma pool at chargen: **TBD** (calibration pending; target: ~80–100 karma gets a competent but not-dominant runner).
- Per-run post-chargen karma gain: **4–7 karma** including notable-choice bonuses.
- Expected raises per run at early game: **1 skill raise or specialization** per 1–2 runs.
- Gritty curve — no easy power fantasy.

## Progression UX

- **Level-up screen:** absent. No "level 5 → 6" moment; advancement happens at the player's choice during Downtime.
- **Character sheet** shows karma balance and available advancements with karma-cost tooltips.
- **Spend moments:** Downtime action ("Train skill", "Meditate for initiation", "Visit teacher to learn spell"). Some advancements require in-game time (training periods) and nuyen (teacher fees, library access, magical lodge time).

## Initiation / metamagic (post-MVP)

- **Initiation grades** unlock metamagics (Masking, Centering, Shielding, Quickening, etc.).
- Each grade costs karma + **ordeal** (fast, meditation, pain, geas, ritual, sacrifice).
- Available only to Awakened characters (mages, shamans, adepts).
- **MVP:** initiation is not implemented; mage PCs max at natural Magic cap during the vertical slice.

## Cyberware upgrades

- Post-chargen cyberware is a **nuyen** purchase plus **in-game time** (surgery + recovery) plus **Essence loss** (permanent).
- Not karma-gated; any runner can chrome up if they can pay.
- Magic rating loss for Awakened characters on Essence drops.

## NSFW / addiction progression crossover

- **BTL and drug addiction** are *negative* progression — they accumulate through use and must be actively managed.
- **Addiction recovery** costs in-game time + nuyen (clinic or cold-turkey willpower tests) — not karma.
- Addiction does not grant karma (no "survival bonus" for overdosing).
