# DESIGN - Cyberware.md

## Decision

**DECIDED:** Cyberware is a first-class subsystem. Four design pillars:

1. **Essence loss is permanent and painful.** No easy recovery.
2. **Full grade / quality tiers.** Used, Standard, Alphaware, Betaware, Deltaware.
3. **Cyberware complications.** Rejection, addiction, mood shifts, cyberpsychosis risk, hidden manufacturer control.
4. **Full visual customization.** Cyberware is visible on-character; styles signal identity and status.

## Essence

- Starting Essence 6.0 per SR convention.
- Each implant reduces Essence by its cost; fractional tracking to 2 decimals.
- Essence affects: Magic rating (awakened), Resonance (technomancer), social impact (stigma/notice), healing rate, spellcasting drain.
- Essence loss is **permanent** — no generic recovery; rare magical/specialist interventions can restore *tiny* amounts at huge cost.
- At Essence ≤ 0 → death / transition out of protagonist role.

## Grades

| Grade | Essence cost | Nuyen cost | Availability |
|---|---|---|---|
| Used | ×1.25 | ×0.75 | Higher (black market) |
| Standard | ×1.00 | ×1.00 | Normal |
| Alphaware | ×0.80 | ×2.00 | Restricted |
| Betaware | ×0.70 | ×4.00 | Hard to find |
| Deltaware | ×0.50 | ×10.00 | Forbidden, clinic-gated |

Numbers follow SR hybrid calibration; will be tuned.

## Complications

Rolled on installation or on trigger events:
- **Rejection** — body rejects implant; requires treatment, temp penalties, may need extraction.
- **Addiction** — wired reflexes / cerebral boosters, simsense abusers; behavioral effects.
- **Mood shifts** — Essence-low characters drift emotionally; can affect relationships.
- **Cyberpsychosis risk** — at very low Essence, detachment and violence risk.
- **Hidden control** — black-market or corp-installed cyberware may have backdoors: manufacturer locks, corp kill-switches, trace signals. Discoverable via the right contacts or after bad events.

## Visual customization

- Cyberware has visible variants: chrome polish, carbon matte, leather sheath, bio-cosmetic disguise.
- Style signals faction / subculture / era (SR1 chunky chrome vs. SR6 seamless bio).
- Others react to obvious cyberware (social modifier in certain venues).

## Installation

- Requires a **clinic** appropriate to the grade.
- Street clinics: higher complication risk, lower cost, faster.
- Corp clinics: lower complication risk, higher cost, SIN required.
- Delta clinics: rare, clinic-specific (Dr. Cross's workshop, certain dragon-owned facilities).
- Installation time: real in-game days; recovery further time.
