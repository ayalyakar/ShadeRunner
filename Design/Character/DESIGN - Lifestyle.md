# DESIGN - Lifestyle

## Decision

**DECIDED:** Full SR classic lifestyle structure with real mechanical weight. Four layers:

1. **Monthly lifestyle tier payment** (Squatter → Luxury).
2. **Detailed bills** on top of tier (rent, food, utilities, fixer retainers).
3. **SIN / credential economy** determining what you can buy legally.
4. **Black market & barter** for the SINless life.

See also `World/DESIGN - Economy.md` for price and availability.

## Lifestyle tiers

| Tier | Monthly | Access | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Street | 0 | None | Sleeping rough, begging, stealing. Heat from police, weather risk. |
| Squatter | 500 | Crash space | Abandoned building, no utilities, no security. |
| Low | 2,000 | Modest apartment | Shared utilities, cheap neighborhood. |
| Middle | 5,000 | Real apartment | Safe building, reliable matrix, basic amenities. |
| High | 10,000 | Quality condo | Doorman, corp-adjacent neighborhoods, access to better contacts. |
| Luxury | 100,000 | Penthouse | Gated, elite, high-society doors open. |

Nuyen numbers are a starting calibration; refine during balance.

## What a lifestyle buys

- **Shelter** — bed, sleep quality (affects recovery).
- **Security** — resistance to being robbed during downtime.
- **Matrix access** — commlink bandwidth, Host proximity.
- **Neighborhood** — who you can meet, whose patrols hit your door.
- **Identity** — listed at this tier's SIN registry?
- **Amenities** — kitchen, gym, armory, garage at higher tiers.

## Itemized bills (optional granularity)

- Rent (dominant).
- Food (can cook to reduce).
- Utilities (power, water, matrix).
- Commlink plan.
- Fixer retainers (keep the calls coming).
- Safehouse upkeep.
- Doc Wagon / medical plan (huge swing if unsubscribed when shot).

Player can opt into detailed bill management or leave it abstracted under the lifestyle tier.

## Lifestyle changes

- **Downgrade**: pay less, but recovery rates drop, heat rises, some contacts become unreachable.
- **Upgrade**: more costly, better recovery, doors open, visibility rises.
- **Missed payment**: grace period, then eviction / cut utilities / contacts cool.
- Payments are **triggered events**, surfaced via commlink, visible in calendar.

## SIN tiers

- **SINless** — no ID. Black market only. Constant risk at checkpoints.
- **Criminal SIN** — stolen or forged, cheap. Unreliable against real checks.
- **Fake SIN Rating 1–6** — paid for, rated by how well it resists scrutiny.
- **Real SIN** — from a corp or government. Legal, traceable, a liability for runners.

Owning multiple fake SINs at different tiers is common and supported.
