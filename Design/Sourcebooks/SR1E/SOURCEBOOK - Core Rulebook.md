## Book-level notes

- First canonical lens on the Sixth World. All later editions' retcons apply
  from their publication year forward; SR1E remains authoritative for
  2050s-era in-game play. See ingestion policy for the timeline-aware
  conflict rule.
- Published 1989 — contains era-specific assumptions (cyberdecks with
  ASIST, pre-Crash-2.0 Matrix, pre-SURGE, pre-Bug-City, Tír Taírngire as a
  young elf state, etc.).
